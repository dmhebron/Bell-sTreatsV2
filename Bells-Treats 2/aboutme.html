My Passion

Baking has always been a passion of mine. In 2016 I had a small baking business that allowed me to take my mind off many of the world's distractions. At the time the name of the business was Sweet Tooth Bakery, however, I have decided to rebrand. My daughter's name is Bella and is often referred to as Bell (a nickname given to her by my mom). She is my motivation and I want to honor how much she has brightened my life.
